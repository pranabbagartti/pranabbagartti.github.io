About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Linguistics unit at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi, working under the supervision of Prof. Samar Husain.


My research interests lie broadly in understanding the underlying mechanisms of sentence processing. Currently, I am investigating agreement attraction errors in Hindi through behavioural experiments to understand how language-specific and cross-linguistic strategies interact during processing.
