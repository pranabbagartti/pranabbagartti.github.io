About:
I am Pranab Bagartti, a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. I belong to the Linguistics unit in the department of Humanities and Social Sciences. I am working with Prof. Samar Husain (lab link). Broadly, I am interested in understanding the underlying mechanisms of sentence processing through behavioural experiments. Currently, I am working on agreement attraction errors in Hindi for the same.
Academic Background:
