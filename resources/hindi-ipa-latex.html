Hindi/Urdu IAST Transliteration in LaTeX

A cheatsheet for linguists working with Hindi interlinear glosses in pdfLaTeX and XeLaTeX

Pranab Bagartti · 2026

§ Required Packages

\usepackage[T1]{fontenc}
\usepackage{lmodern}
These two are sufficient. The accent commands below are built into base LaTeX — no extra package needed. Do not load tipa unless you need IPA symbols elsewhere (it can conflict). Do not load inputenc with XeLaTeX.
